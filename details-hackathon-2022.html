Hackathon 2022

Project information

  • Category: Hackathon
  • Organization: EPHEC
  • Project date: 28 october 2022
  • Value: 10 Hours
  • Real Hours: 48 Hours

Project description: The Hackathon 2022 was a 48-hour event at EPHEC where students could collaborate on a project of their choice. The objective was to develop a project to present to a jury at the event's conclusion. Participating in the Hackathon was a new experience for me, and I thoroughly enjoyed it. Working in groups alongside students I hadn't previously met required us to socialize and collaborate effectively to accomplish our objectives.
